UX Design Lab

Hands-on: user-centered design, accessibility, usability checks

This lab guides you through short interactive exercises that illustrate core UX principles: empathy (persona), usability testing (task success), accessibility (forms & ARIA), and low-fidelity prototyping (wireframe).
